Vuert.js

The headless alerts, notifications & popups library for Vue.js craftsmen.

Why Vuert?

Insanely Easy

Stop struggling in vain!

Done like you would... Designed to be as developer-friendly as possible.
Go straight to the point!

Completely Headless

It's just pure logic!

Vuert comes with no style... Actually, it comes with no renderable elements!
This allows you to define your own components and to style them to match exactly your theme.
Unleash your imagination!

Simply Managed

It's disaster proof!

Say your app enters an infinite loop emitting gazillions of alerts...
Your users won't be overwhelmed with popups and notifications!
Everything will continue working just fine... Even if your app is imploding, under the hood.

Someone said "Debouncing" or "Throttling"?
There will be in future releases!

Don't worry about what happens... Just stay focused on what you do best.
Slee... Ahem... Develop soundly!

Meticulously Typed

100% written in TypeScript!

You know what it's need as an input... You know what you get as an output.
You can't go wrong!
